OUR FOUNDER.

Dr. John Parker Hammond, the ambitious founder of InGen, dreamed of resurrecting the majestic dinosaurs of the past for all to behold. With a spark of genius, he brought these ancient creatures to life on Isla Nublar, crafting a wondrous Jurassic Park. Yet, as nature’s fury unleashed chaos upon his creation, Hammond’s vision turned tragic. Reflecting on his legacy after the park’s downfall, he became a voice for dinosaur preservation, leaving behind a world forever changed by his dream—a testament to both human ingenuity and the untamed power of nature.
